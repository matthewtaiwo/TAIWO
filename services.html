Tee Fist Stitches is a fashion industry where all kinds of sewing tools,fabrices are sold and also where all kind of ready to wear for young and old both male and female are sold also a place where ambitious young dreamers in same line are fully trained.

MISSION

At Tee Fist Stitches, our mission is to empower our customers to feel confident all day,being their best selves by innovating and experimenting with fabrices, materials and styles, so that our customers can have the power and "je ne sais quoi" to succeed in their endeavours. we aim for lifelong partnership with our clients, thats why we provide excellent customer service and a positive shopping experience for customers.
VISION

Our Vision at Tee Fist Stitches is to be a leading influencer in the fashion industry, by setting the standard for high-qulity apparels, affordable clothing and accessories for people of all ages , gender , nationality and ethnicities.
OBJETIVE

1. To identify fashion trends.

2.To showcase quality, well constructed fashions from prominent and cutting-edge designers.

3.To offer a variety of beautiful and high-end fashion accessories.

4.To help everyone learns what clothing and style go best with their unique personalities.

5.To provide quality clothing and customers services at a reasonable price.

6.To achieve profit within the first year.

7.To continually and consistently increase total number of customers per year.

TERMS and CONDITION

1. Orders are validated after initial deopsit of 70% - 100% .

2. Dresses whould be collected at least 2 week after payment .

3. Home /office delivery attracts extra charges .

4. For ready-made wears on display ,check and balance attracts additional fee.

5. Express work attracts extra token.

6. Filling of forms by clients for reference purposes is strongly recommended.

7. We offer bonuses on every referral by clients.

8. No payment;no delivery.

9. In case of adjustment or complaints on dresses, the office should be contacted within maximum 3 days after delivery.
